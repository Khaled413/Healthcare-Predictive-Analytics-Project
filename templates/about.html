About the Heart Disease Prediction System

Project Overview

This system uses machine learning to predict the risk of heart disease based on various health factors.

Heart disease is one of the leading causes of death globally. Early detection and prevention are crucial for reducing mortality rates. This project aims to provide a simple tool that can help identify potential risk factors and encourage early medical consultation.

How It Works

The system uses a Gradient Boosting machine learning model trained on a comprehensive dataset of heart disease cases. The model analyzes 20 different health factors to predict the likelihood of heart disease.

Key features include:

  • Analysis of demographic factors (age, gender)
  • Evaluation of lifestyle factors (exercise, smoking, alcohol consumption)
  • Assessment of medical indicators (blood pressure, cholesterol levels, etc.)
  • Consideration of family history and genetic predisposition

Important Disclaimer

This tool is designed for educational and informational purposes only. It is not intended to replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider regarding any medical conditions or concerns. Never disregard professional medical advice or delay seeking it because of information provided by this system.
